Awesome Subtitle About The Timers Page The Timers page shows all your timers. Start and stop a timer by giving it a long press. Tap a timer to edit it. Click to create a new timer. Awesome Subtitle Creating A Timer Once you have clicked on the Timers screen, you can enter a name for your timer, and choose a color. You can also choose an opposite of the timer, so that when you start your new timer, its opposite will be stopped. For example, you might choose 'busy' as the opposite of 'available', and 'happy' as the opposite of 'sad', so that when you become 'busy' or 'happy', your are no longer 'sad' or 'available'.
